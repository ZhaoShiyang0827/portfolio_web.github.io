Project Overview

Samsung is a company that has prided itself on innovation for the past 78 years. The company's motto is to "Inspire the World, Create the Future" and works to be on top of the latest trends in technology. Their venture into 3D televisions complements this mission in driving society forward, however, the current design of the website that sells their 3D TVs does not communicate this message as well as it should. The redesign will encourage new user traffic and separate Samsung from their competition as innovators and forward thinkers. In addition, this redesign will excite and inform current Samsung 3D TV customers and assure them that they have made the best choice by providing a clean, solid human-centered web experience. Samsung’s five core values to maintain in this project are people, excellence, change, integrity, and co-prosperity.

Purpose of the New Site

The final redesign will serve to better promote Samsung’s 3D TVs. The focus on innovation and a better user experience will create excitement within the target audience and persuade them to purchase a Samsung 3D TVs and give them the confidence that they purchased the best product. Backed by Samsung’s history of product integrity, this will leave a lasting positive impression on both existing and new customers. Not only will this drive sales currently, but it will also increase the likelihood that customers will buy from Samsung again in the future.

Target Audience

Family Night

Potential customers of a Samsung 3D TV can be categorized into two major groups. The first type of potential customers consists of those who want to spend more time with their families and share some engaging, entertaining experiences. They desire an improvement in their TV viewing experience and pursuing a higher quality of life.

Innovators

The second type of potential customers are those geeks who are very enthusiastic about the new technologies. People who are willing to try new things and incorporate the innovative products into their life. Compared with other TV consumers, the target audience for 3D TV are younger and most of them tend to be between 25 and 45 years old. They tend to have higher income to support their pursuits of technologies. With so many different electronic competitors, Samsung does not have a solid loyalty status. With proper direction, this can open a large amount of opportunity for gaining new customers.
